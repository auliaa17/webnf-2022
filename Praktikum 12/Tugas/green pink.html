Park Chanyeol 박찬열
is a South Korean rapper, singer, songwriter, producer, actor and model. He is a member of the South Korean-Chinese boy group Exo, its sub-group Exo-K and sub-unit Exo-SC.
Birth date
November 27, 1992 Seoul, South Korea
2012: Pre-debut
Chanyeol became the last Exo member to be officially introduced to the public on February 23, 2012
2015–present: Acting, songwriting and Exo-SC
In April 2015, Chanyeol made his big screen debut with a supporting role in the South Korean film Salut d'Amour, which starred Park Geun-hyung and Youn Yuh-jung.

Exo (Korean: 엑소; stylized in all caps) is a South Korean-Chinese boy band based in Seoul formed by SM Entertainment in 2011 and debuted in 2012.
